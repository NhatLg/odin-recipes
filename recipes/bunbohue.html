Description

Pho is a internationally beloved Vietnamse dish. However, have you heard of its distant cousin Bun Bo Hue?

Bored of Pho's savouriness and lightness? This dish will be more fragrant and spicy. It's another savoury kick on your palate.

  • 2 lb (907.2 g) beef shank

  • 2 lb (907.2 g) pork hocks
  • 1 lb (453.6 g) Huế style pork sausage chả Huế, which has garlic and whole peppercorns
  • 1 lb (453.6 g) block of pork blood

Steps

  1. Clean the meat
  2. Make the broth
  3. Add spices
