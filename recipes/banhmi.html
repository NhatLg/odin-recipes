Description

Banh Mi is considered a fast food in Vietnam, eating it is sure fast but making will take time

Banh Mi uses barguette as the buns or holder for it's nutritionous content. It is well-balance quick dish to eat on the go

Ingredients

  • 2 lb (907.2 g) beef shank
  • lb (907.2 g) oxtail
    • 2
  • 2 lb (907.2 g) pork hocks
  • 1 lb (453.6 g) Huế style pork sausage chả Huế, which has garlic and whole peppercorns
  • 1 lb (453.6 g) block of pork blood

Steps

  1. Prepare the meat
  2. Put it in barguette
  3. Add vegetables
